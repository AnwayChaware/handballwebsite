About Us

Welcome to the Shree Santa Gajanan Maharaja Handball Club! We are dedicated to promoting the sport of handball and fostering talent at all levels. Our club is a vibrant community of players, coaches, and supporters who share a passion for the game.

Our History

Founded in 15 Aug-2013, our club has a rich history of success and growth. From our humble beginnings to becoming a recognized name in handball, we have always aimed to provide top-notch training and opportunities for players of all ages.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop skilled and passionate handball players while promoting sportsmanship, teamwork, and a healthy lifestyle. We strive to create a positive and inclusive environment where every member can thrive and achieve their full potential.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We are committed to achieving the highest standards in every aspect of the game.
  • Integrity: We believe in fair play and honesty on and off the court.
  • Respect: We value the contributions of every individual and foster a supportive environment.
  • Community: We aim to build strong connections and a sense of belonging among our members.
