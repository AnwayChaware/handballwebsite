ABOUT

The Handball Federation of India (HFI) is the administrative and controlling body for handball, beach handball, and wheelchair handball in the Republic of India. Founded in 1971, HFI is affiliated with the Indian Olympic Association (IOA). HFI is also recognized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India.
